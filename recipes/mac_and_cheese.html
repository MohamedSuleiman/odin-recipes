Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese: A beloved dish made with tender elbow macaroni enveloped in a rich, velvety cheese sauce.

This comforting classic typically includes ingredients like cheddar or American cheese, butter, milk, and sometimes breadcrumbs for a crispy topping.

It's a go-to comfort food enjoyed by all ages."

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Make the Cheese Sauce
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  4. Serve
