Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish originating from Italy, consisting of a round, flat dough base topped with a variety of ingredients.

The dough is typically made from wheat flour, water, yeast, and salt, then rolled out and baked in an oven.

The most common toppings include tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and various meats or vegetables such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers, and olives.

Ingredients

  • Dough (made from wheat flour, water, yeast, salt)
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni (or other meats like sausage, ham, etc.)
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Peppers (like bell peppers)
  • Olives (black or green)
  • Herbs and Spices (like oregano, basil, red pepper flakes)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Preheat your oven
  3. Prepare the pizza peel or baking sheet
  4. Add the cheese
  5. Add your toppings
  6. Bake the pizza
  7. Finish with olive oil
